void SliderJoint::motorEnabled ( bool  enabled)

Set whether or not the motor is enabled.

When the motor is enable the joint will attempt to move at the current set speed up to any limits if they are enabled. The motors actual movement speed is affected by the amount of force it has and the mass of the bodies attached to the joint. A higher forcee is required to move a greater mass.
