Detailed Description

Objects are stored in ObjectGroup layers. Objects may be rectangular, elliptical, polygonal or a polyline. Rectangular and elliptical Objects have their size determined via the AABB, whereas polygon and polyline shapes are defined by a list of points. Objects are rectangular by default.
