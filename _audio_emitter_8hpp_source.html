xy::AudioEmitter
Audio emitter component. Audio emitter provide points within the scene to play audio, either one shot effects or streaming music. If the buffer used to supply the audio is mono, and the scene contains an AudioSystem, then the AudioEmitter will be panned spatially relative to the current listener (usually the active scene camera), unless it has been set to absolute positioning.
